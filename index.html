Featured project
Pathways

Most leadership development programs fail because employees have no shared vocabulary for their own growth before the training begins. One workshop later, nothing has changed.

Pathways is a full L&D ecosystem built around the Korn Ferry Leadership Architect framework. 38 competencies. A shared language for growth across the organization. The program leads with vocabulary because shared definitions are the foundation every other touchpoint depends on.
